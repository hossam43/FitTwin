Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you commit.

Membership
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Coaching
Is coaching included or separate?
Standard includes 1 monthly coach consultation. Elite includes dedicated weekly coaching sessions. Additional private sessions can be booked separately via our Coaches page.
Billing
How does annual billing work?
Pay one year upfront and save 20%. You're billed once yearly and get all features from day one. Cancel within 30 days for a full refund.
Nutrition
How personalized are the nutrition plans?
Standard plans are generated based on your goal, body stats, and dietary restrictions. Elite plans are reviewed and customized by a certified nutrition coach every week.
Avatar
What is the 3D avatar tracker?
Your 3D avatar updates with your real body measurements — showing fat distribution, muscle gain, and transformation progress visually over time. Available on Standard and Elite.
Cancellation
What happens if I cancel?
Your membership remains active until the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no charges after cancellation. Your data is saved for 90 days.
